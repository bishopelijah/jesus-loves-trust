About Us

Jesus Loves Ministries is a registered charitable and educational trust based in West Tambaram, Chennai, dedicated to improving education and opportunities for underprivileged people. Since 1991, we have served people from various vulnerable communities in our region through education, skill development, and social empowerment.

Our Mission

To create opportunities for vulnerable and impoverished communities through education, life skill development, and awareness programs.

Our Vision

A society in which every person has the opportunity to learn and succeed in their areas of interest, irrespective of their social status, caste, or background.

Our Initiatives

  • Educational Support: Free education, study materials, and scholarships for hardworking students.
  • Community Development: Awareness programs, support for senior citizens, and personality development courses.
  • Youth Empowerment: Literacy courses and career mentorship.

Our Impact

Over the years, Jesus Loves Ministries has transformed thousands of lives through awareness programs, education, and social support.
