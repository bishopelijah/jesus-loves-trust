About Us

Jesus Loves Ministries is a Registered charitable and Educational Trust based in West Tambaram, Chennai, dedicated to improving education and opportunities for underprivileged people. Since 1991, we have served people from various vulnerable communities in our region through education, skill development, and social empowerment.

Our Leadership

Founded by Rev. Dr. A. Elijah (Managing Trustee), an ex-serviceman who later became a pastor and was ordained as a bishop in 2024, Jesus Loves Ministries is built on a strong foundation of service and dedication. He has worked full-time, helping underprivileged people through education and social service initiatives. His wife Ms. Elisa plays a key role in assisting and guiding the organization’s mission.

Our Mission

To create opportunities for vulnerable and impoverished communities through education, life skill development, and awareness programs.

Our Vision

A society in which every person has the opportunity to learn and succeed in their areas of interest, irrespective of their social status, caste, or background.

Our Initiatives

  • Educational Support: Free education and study materials, scholarships for hardworking students, and skills/vocational training.
  • Community Development: Awareness programs on health and education, support for senior citizens and orphaned children, and personality development courses.
  • Youth Empowerment: Literacy courses and career mentorship.
